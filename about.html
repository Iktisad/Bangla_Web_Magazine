Mission and Objectives

Our mission is to celebrate and sustain Bengali language and culture globally, fostering connections among the diaspora and promoting cultural exchanges.

  • To encourage and sustain Bengali language classes and other educative efforts for language enthusiasts and the diaspora
  • To promote Bengali as a language of ideation, inquiry, and self-expression
  • To celebrate the lived experiences of Bengalis in international and global spaces

Activities and Goals

Bengali Language Classes and Education

We provide spaces for learning Bengali language that bring the diasporic milieu and other linguistic enthusiasts together, sustaining its everyday forms as a medium of expressing the mundane as well as the imaginative.

Cultural Exchanges and Interactions

Using Bengali as a medium of instruction to teach foreign languages and to interact with other languages through literary-cultural translations, we promote symbiotic coexistence of different diasporic identities.

Celebration of Bengali Cultural Experiences Globally

We acknowledge the existence of Bengalis both outside and inside their territorial borderlands, situating ourselves in the global distribution of Bengalis as a linguo-cultural group.
