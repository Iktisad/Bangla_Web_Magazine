Welcome to Bangla Abhibashi Network, a Glocal Linguo-cultural Association

We are a Montreal-based Bangali diaspora committed to connecting Bengalis worldwide and promoting our rich cultural heritage.

"Bridging Distances, Celebrating Culture"

Call for Contributions

Share your creativity and join us in celebrating Bengali culture! We welcome:

  • Articles: Insights on our annual theme, Bengali culture, and diaspora experiences.
  • Literary Works: Original poetry, short stories, and captivating fiction.
  • Art and Photography: Visual art and photography that tell powerful stories.
  • Personal Narratives: Travel writing, memoirs, and interviews with cultural icons.
  • For any queries email us at info@bangaliabhibasi.org
