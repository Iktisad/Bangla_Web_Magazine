2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They are used to make websites work more efficiently, provide better user experiences, and collect information about how the website is being used.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses only essential cookies necessary for the operation of the website. These include:

  • JWT Authentication Cookies: We use JSON Web Tokens (JWT) to authenticate users. These tokens are stored as cookies on your device and are essential for maintaining secure access to your account and personalized content.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use JWT authentication cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To recognize you when you log in to our website and to maintain your session.
  • Security: To ensure the security of our website and protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • Preferences: To remember your preferences and settings.
