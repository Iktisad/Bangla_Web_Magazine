Write for Us / Share your Art | Photography

We are always seeking fresh perspectives and new voices. Whether you are a seasoned writer or just starting out, we welcome your contributions. If you are an artist or photographer, we would love to feature your work in our magazine. Below are some guidelines to help you get started.

Themes We Are Interested In

  • Bengali culture and traditions
  • Stories of the Bengali diaspora
  • Contemporary issues in Bangladesh
  • Historical perspectives on Bengali heritage
  • Innovations and achievements of Bengalis worldwide
  • Personal narratives and memoirs related to Bengali identity

Submission Guidelines

  • Choose a topic that aligns with our themes of Bengali culture and diaspora experiences.
  • Include a brief bio and a headshot with your submission.
  • Provide a description of your work and its significance to Bengali culture.
  • Contributions are accepted in Bengali, English, and French.
  • All submissions should be in DOCX or PDF format.
  • Submit high-resolution images in JPG or PNG format.
  • Length:
    • Articles: Up to 3000 words.
    • Short Stories: Up to 5000 words.
    • Poetry: Up to 50 lines per poem.
    • Art & Photography: Up to 15 photos.
